Work

Rush Hour

This piece of work represents my version of "Street Style". In this photograph, it captures the hustle and bustle of a train station without any of tthe subjects aknowledging the photographer. This allows for an image to capture a true subject and allows for interpretations in the picture.

Lone Traveler

This photograph portrays a woman who seems to be travelling alone, this image protrays a form of simplicity. The simplicity of this image allows for an array of interpretation.

Doors Closing

This photograph captures a man swifty walking through the door with no one around. His bag falling off his shoulder and the empty train platform allows for interpretation of the idea of the man being in a rush. A rush to make the train as the doors are closing.
